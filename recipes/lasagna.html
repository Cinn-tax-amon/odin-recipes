Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna (plural “lasagne”) is a flat and expanded pasta sheet, traditionally made in Italy with Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan cheese), Béchamel sauce (white sauce), and ragù (a meat-based sauce). Despite the traditional form still being popular, lasagna has taken on many different recipes over the years.

Homemade Lasagna is a classic that every cook should have in their rotation. Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • Ricotta Cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoonspepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 lb ground italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Equipment

  • one 9 x 13-inch casserole dish
